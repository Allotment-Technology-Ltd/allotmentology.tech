## restormel_keys_summary

Restormel Keys is a drop-in BYOK layer for AI-powered applications. It is designed to solve two recurring builder problems: routing requests across multiple AI providers through a consistent interface, and allowing end-users to bring their own API keys inside the builder’s product.

Its core promise is production-grade key management, provider routing, and cost controls that builders can ship in hours instead of weeks. The product is intended for solo founders, AI SaaS builders, open-source maintainers, and small product teams who need provider flexibility, user BYOK, and business logic such as entitlement and budget control without building it all themselves.

The strongest differentiator is that it combines embeddable end-user BYOK, provider routing, and cost or entitlement logic in one coherent, lightweight package.

## standard_answer_why_now

The timing is right because AI-powered products are moving from experimentation into operational use, and the infrastructure gaps are becoming more painful. Teams building AI applications increasingly need multi-provider routing, cost controls, entitlement logic, and secure end-user BYOK support, but the available options are either too heavy, too enterprise-oriented, or still require teams to build security-sensitive workflows themselves.

The opportunity now is to extract, simplify, and commercialise what has already proved useful in practice rather than inventing a platform from scratch.

## standard_answer_what_problem_exists

Teams building AI-powered applications repeatedly run into the same problems: they need to work across multiple providers, manage model choice and cost, control entitlements, and increasingly let users bring their own API keys. Today, many teams solve this badly: by relying on heavyweight infrastructure tools, expensive enterprise platforms, or fragile custom implementations.

The specific gap Restormel Keys targets is the lack of a lightweight, product-friendly layer that combines embeddable BYOK, provider routing, and cost or entitlement logic in one coherent package. That forces builders to recreate difficult, security-sensitive, and operationally messy logic themselves.

## standard_answer_what_differentiates_the_product

Restormel Keys is differentiated by both what it does and how it is delivered. It combines provider routing, end-user BYOK, cost estimation and tracking, entitlement logic, and embeddable UI in a library-first package that is easier for product teams to adopt than heavyweight proxy or gateway approaches.

It is also differentiated by its origin. The product is not speculative. It is being extracted from SOPHIA, where the underlying capabilities have already been used in a real application context. That gives it a stronger practical grounding than tools built purely from market positioning.
